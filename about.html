Our Story

A Legacy Built on Trust & Transparency

RIYA MOTORS was founded over 18 years ago with a single vision: to provide the people of Siddhpur with honest, professional, and affordable automobile care — the kind of service you'd give your own family.

What started as a modest workshop has grown into Siddhpur's premier multi-brand car care center. Today, we are equipped with modern hydraulic lifts, advanced diagnostic tools, and a team of skilled mechanics who take pride in their craft.

"We believe that every customer deserves to understand exactly what is being done to their vehicle and why. Transparency is not just a policy for us — it is a promise."

From routine servicing to complex engine overhauls, from genuine spare parts to emergency breakdowns — RIYA MOTORS is the complete solution trusted by thousands of vehicle owners across Siddhpur and the surrounding region.

Our Journey

Growing With Siddhpur

From a small workshop to Siddhpur's most trusted car care center — our journey has been driven by passion and your trust.

2006

The Beginning

RIYA MOTORS opened its doors in Siddhpur with a small team, big ambitions, and an unwavering commitment to honest automobile service.

2010

Expanding Services

Added comprehensive AC service & repair capabilities, cooling system specialists, and a dedicated spare parts inventory for fast turnaround.

2015

Multi-Brand Expertise

Expanded our technical capabilities to service all major automobile brands — from Maruti Suzuki and Hyundai to Toyota, Mahindra, Tata, and more.

2018

Hydraulic Lift Facility

Installed our professional hydraulic lift system — a major upgrade enabling precise underbody inspections, safer repairs, and more accurate diagnostics.

2022

1000+ Vehicles Milestone

Proudly crossed 1000+ vehicles serviced, a testament to the trust our community has placed in us over the years. We introduced pickup & drop service.

Today

Siddhpur's #1 Car Care Center

With a 4.7-star rating, 4 expert mechanics, and over 18 years of trust — RIYA MOTORS continues to serve Siddhpur with the same passion and integrity we started with.
